L1–L4 – THE CHESS BOARD

A CLEAR MAP OF WHO DOES WHAT – AND WHY IT MATTERS

The KONTROL world model treats your organization like a chess board. Each layer plays a different role: leadership, system design, day‑to‑day execution, and specialist support. Together, they turn strategy into governed, measurable action without drowning your team in complexity.

This document explains what each level does and why it exists – in language you can share with clients and partners.

L1 – OVERWATCH

REF_ID: L1-ORIGIN
LAYER: LEADERSHIP

WHAT L1 DOES

L1 is where human leadership lives. It is the layer where owners and executives decide what matters, which risks are acceptable, and where the company is trying to go. Decision‑making sits here – not in the models.

  • Sets direction and priorities for the whole board.
  • Approves or rejects major moves and commitments.
  • Holds final responsibility for outcomes.

WHY L1 EXISTS

Every system needs a clear “King” on the board. L1 ensures that all automation, advice and tooling stay aligned with human intent. It keeps accountability where it belongs: with the people who own the business, not the software that supports it.

WHAT L2 DOES

L2 translates leadership intent into structure. It decides how products, data, automations and governance should behave so that every new feature or workflow lands in a predictable place on the board.

WHY L2 EXISTS

Without L2, systems drift into one‑off solutions that are hard to maintain. The Elders keep the architecture coherent so that every change strengthens the platform instead of fragmenting it.

L3 – THE FORGE (WORKING NODES)

OPERATIONS_MATRIX
REF_ID: L3-FORGE
LAYER: AGENCY & PRODUCT DEPARTMENTS

CLIENT MANAGEMENT (OLLIE‑ASSISTED)

Handles onboarding, day‑to‑day communication, contact reports and weekly updates, with AI support for drafting and summarising so humans can focus on decisions.

STRATEGY & INSIGHTS

Combines research, data and planning into clear briefs and roadmaps that guide campaigns and product work.

CREATIVE & BRAND SYSTEMS

Turns strategy into concepts, copy, design and content that stay on‑brand across every touchpoint.

PROJECT MGMT & COMPLIANCE

Coordinates timelines, resources and vendors, while making sure campaigns and events respect the rules that apply to them.

SERVICES (LEGAL / FINANCE / HR / ADMIN / GOVERNANCE)

Provides the internal backbone: contracts, invoicing, payroll, hiring and internal governance that keep the agency healthy.

THE FORGE (PRODUCT & APP STUDIO)

Builds and maintains the digital tools and child products that support clients – from dashboards to verification flows and specialist consoles.

WHY L3 EXISTS

L3 is where plans turn into work. By separating client management, strategy, creative, delivery, services and product building, the Forge makes it clear who owns which part of the journey – and how AI can support without taking over.
